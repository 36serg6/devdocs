Manage company credit

The company credit entity operates with the following attributes:

  • Credit limit
  • Available credit
  • Outstanding balance

The credit limit is allocated by merchant, while available credit and outstanding balance are automatically calculated by the system based on the customer transactions (place an order, return) and merchantâ€™s transactions (refund, reimburse, update credit limit, cancel order).

Manage company credit limits

When you create a company, the credit limit is set to 0. Use the PUT /V1/companyCredits/:id call to change this value and perform other updates to the companyâ€™s credit settings.
